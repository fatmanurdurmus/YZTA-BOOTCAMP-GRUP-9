# CarbonPilot AI Project Brief

## Vision

CarbonPilot AI helps Turkish industrial exporters, starting with demir-celik producers, prepare for CBAM/SKDM obligations by turning messy operational data into validated carbon accounting, compliance evidence, and carbon-cost intelligence.

## Core Problem

Exporters often hold carbon-relevant data across ERP logs, invoices, shipment PDFs, spreadsheets, and supplier documents. Manual extraction and calculation are slow, error-prone, and hard to audit. CBAM/SKDM increases the financial cost of incorrect or late reporting.

## Product Promise

CarbonPilot AI provides a SaaS-style workflow that:

- ingests operational documents and structured activity data;
- extracts candidate carbon parameters with AI;
- validates all data with strict schemas;
- calculates Scope 1, Scope 2, and CBAM-focused Scope 3 emissions with deterministic code;
- retrieves legal/factor references through Law-RAG;
- audits outputs with a Critic Agent;
- generates PDF/JSON-ready compliance reports;
- simulates green transformation scenarios and carbon-cost savings.

## MVP User Groups

- Export manager: uploads documents and tracks compliance readiness.
- Environmental engineer: checks activity data, factors, and calculation results.
- Sustainability director: compares investment and energy transition scenarios.
- Auditor: reviews evidence, sources, and report outputs.
- Facility manager: monitors hotspots and risk indicators.

## MVP Vertical

The first demo vertical is demir-celik. This keeps the scope focused while still showing high CBAM relevance, direct emissions, electricity emissions, purchased-input emissions, and upstream logistics.
